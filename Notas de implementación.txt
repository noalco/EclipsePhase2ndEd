		- Criticals:			X

	- MENTAL ARMOR
		- The Psi Armor and Psi Shield sleights provide mental armor. Like physical armor, this mental armor reduces the amount of damage inflicted by a psychic assault.
	- DURATION
		- Constant: Constant sleights are always “on.”
		- Instant: Instant sleights inflict an immediate and permanent effect that lasts without effort.
		- Temporary: Temporary sleights last for a limited duration with no extra effort. The temporary duration is determined your WIL ÷ 5 and is measured in action turns, minutes, or hours, as noted.
		- Sustained: Sustained sleights last as long as you want, but require active effort and concentration: apply a –10 modifier to all other skill tests while the sleight is sustained. The async must also stay within Close range + 10 meters of the target, otherwise the sleight immediately ends.
	- INFECTION MODIFIERS
		- Whenever you use psi-gamma sleights, two things happen:			X
			- your Infection Rating increases by that sleight’s Infection modifier			X
			- the exovirus must make an Infection Test:			X
				- The target number for your Infection Test equals your current Infection Rating			X
				- If this test fails, nothing happens, and you pull off the sleight without any negative effects			X
				- If it succeeds, you suffer an influence effect appropriate to the sub-strain			X
				- Critical Failure: You are immune to influence effects until your next recharge.			X
				- Superior Success: Apply a +1 modifier to the influence effect roll per superior success.			X
				- Critical Success: an influence effect is chosen and the GM also picks one of the following:			X
					- Checkout Time
					- Interference
		- THE INFECTION'S INFLUENCE			X
			- Roll 1d6 and consult your sub-strain’s influence effects, applying +1 per superior success rolled on the Infection Test.			X
		- PUSHING SLEIGHTS
			- Your Infection Modifier doubles and you automatically suffer DV 1d6
			- Choose one of the following effects for your pushed sleight:
				- Increased Range: Range doubles. This effect is cumulative with the range increase against other asyncs
				- Increased Effect: Any modifiers provided by the sleight are doubled.
				- Increased Power: The sleight is resisted by WIL Check ÷ 2.
				- Increased Penetration: Psi Shield armor is reduced by half.
				- Increased Duration: Double the sleight’s Duration (temporary sleights only).
				- Extra Target: You may affect one extra target with the sleight.
			- You may also push already active psi-chi sleights. increase your Infection Rating by 5 and make an Infection Test. Psi-chi pushes are temporary, lasting only WIL ÷ 5 minutes.
		- INFECTION RATING THRESHOLDS
			- Infection Rating 33+: Apply the Increased Effect push effect to all of your psi-chi sleights.
			- Infection Rating 66+: Apply one free push effect to all of your psi-gamma sleights. The same effect must be applied to each.
		- EASING THE INFECTION
			- For every short recharge action you take, reduce your Infection Rating by 10.
			- It may not be decreased lower than your base Infection Rating (Psi Level × 10).
			- For every long recharge, reset your Infection to its base rating.
			- You may not reduce your Infection Rating if you have active influence effects — the urge must be satiated first.
